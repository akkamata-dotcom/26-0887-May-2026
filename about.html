About Bus booking

Bus booking is a trusted bus booking platform connecting passengers to destinations across Kenya. We make travel simple, affordable, and reliable.

Our Mission

To provide every Kenyan with a fast, easy, and affordable way to book bus tickets from the comfort of their home or phone.

Why Choose Bus booking?

  • Wide range of routes across Kenya
  • Affordable and transparent pricing
  • Easy online booking — no queues
  • Multiple departure times to fit your schedule
  • Reliable and punctual service

Our Story

Bus booking was founded with one goal — to make bus travel in Kenya stress-free. We noticed how much time people wasted standing in long queues at bus stations, and we decided to change that. Today, Bus booking serves thousands of passengers every week across the country.

Our Values

  • Reliability — We show up on time, every time.
  • Affordability — Fair prices for every Kenyan.
  • Simplicity — Booking a ticket should take seconds, not hours.
  • Safety — Passenger safety is our top priority.
